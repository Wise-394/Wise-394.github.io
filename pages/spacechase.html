Space Chase

Space Chase is a spaceship 2D shooting game that will test your skills and reflexes! In this fast-paced adventure, you must take control of your spacecraft and navigate through space, battling waves of enemy ships that are out to get you.

TechStack

These are the tools I used to develop this project:

Unity Game Engine
• I developed and designed Space Chase using Unity game engine
C#
• The game is fully coded in C#, handling gameplay mechanics, enemy AI, and physics interactions.
Aseprite
• I used Aseprite to create and animate the game's pixel art assets, ensuring a retro and visually appealing style.
